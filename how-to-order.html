how to order



here's how to order from me ᓚᘏᗢ


1. choose what item you want to add to your collection!


2. send me a message via instagram/whatsapp (find them here) to place your order. your message should include:

• product name

• colour

• your size (include any alterations you'd like, e.g. longer sleeves)


3. before i start making your order, i will confirm that you are happy with the colour i have/will order and i will take payment via bank transfer when i finish your order.

shipping is included in the price (royal mail tracked 48), and i will need your email so you'll be able to track your package!


4. i aim to create and send orders within a week, so hang tight and thank you for purchasing! ヾ(≧▽≦*)o


side note: i'm in my final year so it may take longer to create and deliver orders, apologies in advance and i'll let you know if i'm running late with them!

thank you for your understanding ♡
